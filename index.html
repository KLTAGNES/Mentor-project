How to: create a safe & easy-to-remember password

START

Incorrect password

✖ Do not use short, simple passwords.

✖ Do not use date of birth, your pet’s name or personal information.

✖ Do not writing your passwords down on a note(it is not too safe).

✖ Some online banking uses Two-Factor authentication (2FA) automatically. Try it!

Correct password

✔ Longer passwords are better passwords.

✔ Use favorite quote or song,the first letter of each syllable, using capital letters.

✔ Replacing some of the letters with similar-looking numbers, special characters "TR0UI3L3".

✔ Memorable passwords “RedPantsTree4!”, "YummiP3pperoni!","Beartrap.is.DANG3R"

✔ Now, it is your turn! Write some strong password!
